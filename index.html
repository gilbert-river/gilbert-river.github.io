This website is a prototype "learning-focused digital twin", intended to help organise and accumulate knowledge about the Gilbert River Agricultural Precinct, between the Green Hills and Chadshunt stations. In this prototype, the focus is on water.

Water availability
Options for accessing water in the dry season
Farm dams
Gully dams and ring tanks
Water management
Water licences and approval processes
Gilbert River
Bedsands, flooding, and fish
Vegetation management
Regulations related to clearing of vegetation
Green Hills Dam Proposal
Information about the proposed dam
Etheridge Shire Ag Precinct
An initiative to support ag production

Where a Formula 1 racing team might improve performance using a "high-fidelity" digital twin to combine real-time data and computer modeling, digital twins of river catchments and agriculture have to aim to progressively improve understanding over time.

This website was set up as a prototype portal bringing together knowledge about the region. Please note it was last updated in 2022.
