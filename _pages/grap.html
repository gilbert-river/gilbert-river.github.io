The Digital Twin for the Gilbert River Agricultural Precinct spans the properties along the Gilbert River from Green Hills to Chadshunt stations. This is an area in which agricultural development has been pursued over many years, including through use of bedsands in the Gilbert River, exploration of a possible Green Hills Dam, and Etheridge Agricultural Precinct.
